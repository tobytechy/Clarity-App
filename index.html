Common questions
Can I switch plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your billing period, then move to the free plan. Your data is never deleted.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.
Do Village members need an account?
They get an invite link and can join without paying anything. Only the person in recovery needs a subscription.
